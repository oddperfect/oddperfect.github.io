NTK Eats
Our first NTK application.

Going out to eat? You need to know restaurant inspections to help you decide where to eat. This information has always been on a web site or in an application that you have to update constantly to get the latest inspections. Now with NTK Eats inspections come directly from the cities so it is always up to date. To only see the restaurants that meet the level of 'cleanliness' you want, filter the inspections right on the map. You even can look at a restraurant's previous inspections. Now available in iTunes.

NTK Eats shows restaurant inspections for:

  • Chicago
  • New York State
  • Boston
  • Seattle
  • Austin
  • Oakland
  • Montgomery County, MD
